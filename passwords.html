Passwords

Every information system makes use of some sort of authentication, and passwords are the most common form of security. Millions of people make use of passwords per day. Thus, it is extremly important to ensure that the protection mechinism is sufficient. There are various methodfs to secure passwords, these will be discussed below.

Password Length

The length of your password is extremely important. Using longer passwords make cracking it much more difficult. Eight (8) character long passwords were seen as a respectable strandard for quite a while, but as processing power is constantly increasing, 10 or even 12 charcters are becoming necessary.

Password Characters

In addition to password length, it is important to strengthen your passwords by using various characters. These should be non repeating and include numbers as well as symbols. This can help prevent a brute-force attack.
